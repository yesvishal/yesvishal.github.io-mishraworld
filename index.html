What are top secret honeymoon destinations

Why go for a cliched honeymoon when you can plan a secret one...

Carate, Costa rica

A peaceful beach place from where you can visit Corcavado national park along with the experience of beautiful rainforest.

Flight
It is 194 km from Juan Santamaria International airport,San Jose,Costa Rica. One can book a flight from Mumbai to San Jose.
Hotels
Lookout inn lodge is better with ratings also you can go to Finca Exotica Eco lodge. Both are 3 star hotels.

Sapa, Vietnam

This place is famous for ‘Heaven’s gate’ and ‘Silver waterfall’ in Hoanq Lien national park. There is a local market where you can buy cool stuffs if you have good bargaining skills.

Flight
One can book a flight from Mumbai to Hanoi and can go by train or domestic flight to Sapa.
Hotels
BB hotel Sapa,Sapa panorama hotel,Sapa elite hotel are some of the best places to stay.

Tanzania

An african country having a rich wildlife of african lion,african leopard,cape buffalo,african elephant and rhinoceros. The safari ride is an unforgettable experience.

Flight
You can fly with Qatar airways or Etihad Airways from Bengaluru to Dar Es Salaam.
Hotels
There are many options for good hotels like Park Hyatt Zanzibar,For season safari lodge and if you want to feel the place go for Singita sabora tented camp or Mwiba lodge.
